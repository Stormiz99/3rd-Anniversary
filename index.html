It all started 3 years ago... The moment our eyes met, something in my heart just clicked.

Every laugh we shared has become a cherished memory I hold close, a constant reminder of the genuine joy we found in each other’s company.

I realized I wanted to be a permanent part of your life.🤍

Every step forward was better because you were beside me. Looking back, I realize that the path didn't matter as much as the person walking it with me.

They say time flies when you’re having fun, but with you, time flies because I’ve finally found where I belong. These three years have gone by in the blink of an eye, and if I could, I’d hit pause on every single moment just to stay in your presence a little longer.

Every second spent with you feels like a beautiful dream I never want to wake up from, a quiet space where time seems to stand still. ✨

I love planning surprises just to see that look on your face. Seeing you light up with a genuine smile is—and always will be—my favorite view in the world. ❤️

Your smile is the only 'thank you' I ever need.

You are my best friend and my home.🏠

In the symphony of life, our hearts find the sweetest melody.🎶

Your happiness is my happiness. Always.

I’ve always thought light colors suit you best—they bring out your softness and make you look even more radiant.

I love that with you, We don’t always have to act like an adult who has everything figured out. We can be silly, laugh at random things, and just enjoy the moment without worrying about being serious all the time.
